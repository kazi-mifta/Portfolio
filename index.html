Measure Arch Height differene from foot scans
Miftahul, Kazi
U.S. Provisional patent application, filed April 3, 2023

Using scans of load-bearing and non-load-bearing foot, measure the arch height difference. It is an indicator of pronation of the foot. It also helps to determine the mobility and flexibility of the foot's arch. This is valuable in several contexts, including sports medicine, physical therapy, and biomechanical assessments.

Sensors, 2022

A mobile application is developed to measure the dimensions of the foot from images and 3D scans. It was designed for patients who would send their foot scans to pedorthists and get effective treatments.
Foot Dimesnisions Measurement and Landmarks Segmentaion
Kazi Miftahul Hoque
Structure, Structure Cloud Operations

We designed several key algorithms to localize the 1st and last metatarsal of the foot. We also developed algorithms to measure the width, length, and ball girth of the foot. All of these algorithms are combined and deployed to the cloud. Rigorous testing was performed using a dataset of 1 million foot scans. The performance was great as it worked on foot scans with noises, holes, etc.
Foot Scan Quality Indicator
Kazi Miftahul Hoque
Structure, Structure SDK

We developed an interactive indicator to help take better foot scans. While scanning the foot, it would slowly go from red to green. It would assist the user in better gauging the quality of the mesh they are capturing. The heel region and plantar region of the foot are critical for orthotics development. If there are holes in these regions then the indicator will not go green.This feature is included into the Structure SDK.
